TimoBlends

    Hi! I'm Timo Veijalainen, also known as TimoBlends, a 22-year-old self-taught barber. I started cutting hair for my friends at home and in the military, and it quickly became my passion. I'm always learning and and studying to enhance my skillset.
    Additionally, I recently won 1st place in the Hair Cup Finland 2024 Fade competition for junior barbers. Excited to work at FedeCollective where I bring more than just a haircut; I bring confidence and good vibes. Hopefully I see you in the chair!
